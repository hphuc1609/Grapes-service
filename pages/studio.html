Founded in 1996, Paradise is a multi award winning design consultancy specialising in commercial Interior Design and Architecture within the leisure, hospitality and retail sectors. Located in Shoreditch and with a diverse portfolio, Paradise prides itself upon creating exciting and indifferent interiors setting the bench mark for future trends and customer experiences.

“The team is extremely talented with formidable experience and commitment to each individual project. Winning awards from both the hospitality and retail sectors has given Paradise is recognised for our dedication for work that we have conducted throughout the past decades,” Darren Paradise - Creative Director
